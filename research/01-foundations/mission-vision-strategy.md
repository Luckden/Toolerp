# Mission, Vision, Strategy, and Goals

Observed pattern:

- Mission and vision describe why the enterprise exists and what future state it seeks.
- Strategy describes choices, constraints, and resource allocation for achieving that future state.
- Business goals translate strategy into measurable outcomes.

Graph model:

- mission -> informs -> vision
- vision -> constrains -> strategy
- strategy -> operationalizes -> goals

Interpretation:

- These are not operational hierarchies by themselves.
- They are intent layers that constrain governance, funding, architecture, and execution.

Source status:

- Framework-specific and practitioner convention.
