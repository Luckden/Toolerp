# TOGAF, ArchiMate, and Zachman

TOGAF:

- Source type: official standards body.
- Interpretation: architecture methodology and framework.
- Useful for: architecture practice organization, content structure, and ADM-style progression.

ArchiMate:

- Source type: official standards body.
- Interpretation: modeling language.
- Useful for: expressing business, application, and technology relationships as a semantic model.

Zachman:

- Provisional interpretation: classification taxonomy rather than a delivery method.
- Needs direct source confirmation in the next research pass.

Model implication:

- Frameworks and languages should not be conflated.
- A method tells you how to work.
- A language tells you how to model.
- A taxonomy tells you how to classify.
